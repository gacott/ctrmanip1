What is a CTR manipulation service?

CTR manipulation service increases a website’s or web page’s visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) by manipulating the click-through rate (CTR).

How does CTR manipulation work?

CTR manipulation works by targeting keywords related to a website’s content and using various strategies such as search engine optimization, pay-per-click advertising, and other techniques to boost the click-through rate for those keywords.

What are the benefits of using a CTR manipulation service?
